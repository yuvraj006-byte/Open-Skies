?? GAME SYSTEM DOCUMENT (Structured Version) 

1. GAME START FLOW
1.1 Username Selection
* Player chooses a username.
* Username is saved for the session.
1.2 Story Option
* Player chooses:
o Read Story
o Skip Story

2. STARTING SEQUENCE
2.1 Continent Selection
Player selects one of the following continents:
* Kamino
* Mustafar
* Geonosis
* Keitum
* Arkania
* Nepotis
2.2 Region Assignment
* After selecting a continent, player is randomly assigned a Region from the database.
2.3 Sky Stables Assignment
* Player is randomly assigned a Sky Stable in the selected country.

3. REGION CLASSIFICATION SYSTEM
Each country belongs to one of three region types:

3.1 GREEN Region
* Safest region.
* Lowest enemy encounter rate.
* Lowest treasure quality.
* Very rare Lucky Encounters.
* Strong Enemy Encounters are very rare.
3.2 RED Region
* 40% more dangerous than GREEN.
* Higher enemy encounter rate.
* Stronger enemies than GREEN.
* Better loot.
* Higher chance of items also found in GREEN.
* Rare Lucky Encounters.
* Strong Enemy Encounters are rare.
3.3 BLACK Region
* 60% more dangerous than GREEN.
* Strongest enemies.
* Best loot.
* Contains World Bending treasures.
* Lucky Encounters are common.
* Strong Enemy Encounters are common.

4. ENEMY SYSTEM
4.1 Enemy Assignment
* Each region has a predefined enemy pool.
* Enemies are randomly selected from the pool.
* The level and difficulty of the enemies is defined in accordance to the regions.
4.2 Enemy Scaling
* Enemy level corresponds to region type:
o GREEN ? Low Level
o RED ? Medium Level
o BLACK ? High Level

5. TREASURE SYSTEM
5.1 Treasure Assignment
* Each region has a predefined treasure pool.
* Treasures are randomly selected.
5.2 Treasure Quality
* Every Region has four types of loot:
o Common, Rare, Very Rare and Unique.
* Every Region's loot's quality will depend on the region.

o Green --> All loot types will be useless for Black Region and comparable to OR only a little better than Red Region's loot.

o Red --> All loot types will be better than Green Region OR Basic ones comparable to Green Region's Unique Loot. And useless for Black Region and comparable to OR only a little better than Black Region's loot.

o Black --> All loot types will be better than Green and Red Region OR Basic ones comparable to Red Region's Unique Loot.

6. PLAYER STARTING CONDITIONS
After region assignment:
* Player receives:
o Some amount of money.
o Basic equipment (GREEN level Common equipment).

7. FLYING DATABASE & SYSTEM
7.1 Flying Mount Usage
* Player can use Flying Mount system to travel.
* Each flight triggers a cooldown.
* During cooldown, player cannot fly.
7.2 Flight Upgrades
Player can upgrade flight system to:
* Increase flight range.
* Reduce cooldown time.
* Reduce fuel consumption.
* Increase frequency of flights.
* Get better Mounts.

8. PLAYER ACTIONS
The player can:
* Choose username.
* Choose starting continent.
* Accept or reject rewards.
* Accept or reject side quests.
* Choose to attack enemy.
* Choose to befriend enemy.
* Quit the game.
* Use the flight system.
* Use items.

9. LUCKY ENCOUNTERS SYSTEM
9.1 Definition
* Chance to double loot from treasure chest.
9.2 Requirements
Locked behind difficult:
* Mathematics questions
* Physics questions
* English questions
9.3 Difficulty
* Harder than side quest questions.
9.4 Region Frequency
THIS IS SUBJECT TO CHANGE IN ACCORDANCE TO THE DIFFICULTY LEVEL OF THE GAME.
* GREEN Common
* RED Rare
* BLACK Very Common

10. SIDE QUEST SYSTEM
10.1 Content
* Mathematics questions
* Physics questions
* English questions
10.2 Trigger
* Random chance.
* Probability varies by region (to be decided in group discussion).

11. FINAL BOSS SYSTEM
11.1 Boss Characteristics
* Monster-type enemy.
* Fixed power value.
* Fixed health value.
* Deals fixed damage to player.
* Receives limited amount of damage from the player.
11.2 Win Condition
* Player must defeat the Final Boss.
* For this Version of the game the Final Boss will be Lucifer.
* And the next one would have Lilith as the Final Boss, the mother of all demons.

12. GAME END CONDITIONS
The game ends when:
1. Player dies.
2. Player quits.
3. Player defeats the Final Boss.
